FAQ's

What is a stock?

A stock, at the most basic level, is a piece of a company that you can buy.

What is a mutual fund?

A mutual fund is a collection of stocks, bonds, currencies, and other securities that are collected into a large, ‘mutual’ portfolio that you can buy in to.

Can I use email id to login?

No. Userid is required to login.

When can you start the game?

When five participants are available in lobby, game will start automatically.
